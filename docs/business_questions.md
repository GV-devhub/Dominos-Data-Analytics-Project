# Business Questions for Domino’s Pizza Analytics Project

This project is driven by real-world business questions that a pizza chain like Domino’s would ask to improve sales, operations, and customer experience.

---

## 1. Sales Performance

- How are total sales trending daily, weekly, and monthly?
- Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
- What is the average order value (AOV)?
- Which pizza sizes (Small, Medium, Large) contribute most to revenue?

---

## 2. Customer Ordering Behavior

- What times of day have the highest order volume?
- Which days of the week show peak demand?
- Are there seasonal or monthly patterns in ordering?

---

## 3. Product & Menu Insights

- Which pizza categories (Veg, Non-Veg, Premium) perform best?
- Which pizzas are underperforming and may need promotion or removal?
- How do ingredients influence customer preferences?

---

## 4. Operational Efficiency

- Are there predictable rush hours that require more staffing?
- Which pizzas require more preparation time (if data available)?
- How can stores optimize inventory based on demand patterns?

---

## 5. Marketing & Strategy

- Which pizzas should be promoted based on revenue and popularity?
- What bundles or combos could increase average order value?
- How can customer behavior insights support targeted campaigns?

---

## 6. Security & Access Control (RBAC)

- Which roles need access to which reports?
- How can sensitive data be restricted based on job function?
- How does least-privilege access apply to analytics?

---

These questions guide the SQL analysis, Excel dashboard, and business recommendations included in this project.
